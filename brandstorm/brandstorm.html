BACKGROUND

This project provided me the opportunity to learn what's it like to be a product manager. It was also an entry for L'Oreal Brandstorm 2012 marketing competition. My team and I won the National Championship in China, ranked 1/237. The challenge was to design a new sub-brand for YUESAI, an established skin-care and make-up brand, to help targeting Chinese young women aged 18-25.

Market Research

Market Size & Market Share

China grows nearly 8 percent annually in cosmetic and personal care consumption, which is much faster than the global average. Skincare and makeup contribute 72%. There're 100 million young ladies aged from 18 to 25 in China. The number almost equals to the population of whole Japan.

The Trend of Affordable Luxury

And for the luxury, the products for these young ladies grow by a double digit each year in China, whereas the other countries declined. China is the youngest luxury market now. Affordable Luxury is becoming our young ladies' new appetite.

Middle-class Princess

The ladies we face are the generation under one child policy, they are the princess in the booming middle-class family in China. They want to be beautiful of course, but never sacrifice health. They are the college student and office novice. They like "nude makeup", and the product BB cream grows by that amazing rate within just a few years.

Concept&Product

Trandtional Chinese Medicine

We name the YUESAI's sub-brand Yan Fang. Yan means face, or looks. Fang means TCM recipe, or prescription. It is tailormade skin solution for every young lady. As YUESAI's sub-brand, YAN FANG inherits TCM technology and expertise in Chinese skin from YUESAI.

Have Fun & Do It My Way

At the same time, YAN FANG stays close to its young consumers. What our young ladies want is "different from others", "fun" and "always changing". So, Yan Fang should offer them fresh experience which is fun and unique. They know they're different, and they know how to deal with their difference. So we need a personalized solution. and The key is "Have Fun & Do it my way!"

Mix & Match

The following is our product concept. On the back of the first picture is the basic BB cream, and on the right are the cubes. These cubes are our TCM recipe. They contain cream with different functions. Consumers can choose any cubes out of any purpose. and it's very easy to use.
First, press some basic BB cream onto your palm.
Next, use your fingertip to take some cream from the cubes you choose.
Then rub all these cream evenly in your palm.
Finally, apply the mixture to your face.

Cubes Catagories

  • Colouring cubes: for adjusting skin tone, correcting yellowish or pale skin color.
  • Texture cubes: for improving skin quality,like hydrating, whitening, etc.
  • Fragrance cubes: for natural smell and good mood.
  • Theme cubes: for special occasions like maskerada.
  • Lifestyle cubes: for bad living habits. Young ladies would like to live a healthy life, but they break the rules from time to time. lifestyle cubes are remedies for such little mistakes as staying up late, or too much spicy food.
