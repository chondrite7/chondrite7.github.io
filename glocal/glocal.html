Glocal

Entrepreneurship, Business Model, Data Analytics

BACKGROUND

This was an entrepreneur project that I did in grad school. It was also my first startup pitch to real investors. Glocal is a database-enabled business solution that helps connect local merchants and online shoppers. I played the role of the business/marketing person in a team of five.

The Pain Points

Alex and Haley's Problems

Alex and Hailey have a final exam tomorrow.
Hailey doesn't have the textbook yet, but it's checked out at the library and she doesn't have time to wait for Amazon. There are a few local bookstores, but she's not sure if they carry the book she needs.
Alex, on the other hand, doesn't worry about the exam. She's more interested in finding a green dress for the traffic light party she's going to after the exam. Those branded dresses in the downtown shopping center are too expansive and not special enough for her, so she's thinking about some local designer dresses, but she has no clue where to start.

Glocal to the Rescue!

How Glocal helps shoppers and local merchants

  • For online shoppers, Glocal provides locally available options when they're searching for goods online.
  • For local small business owners, Glocal provides a cloud-based inventory management tool to help build their online visibility and expand their customer base.

The figure on the left demonstrates how Glocal works.

  • From the shopper's end, it is a web-browser extension that pops up local alternatives when the shopper is searching on shoppable websites. The user will be asked to share location, preferred distance, preferred price range, etc. in the extension setting to make sure the pop-up local alternative is desirable.
  • From the local merchants' end, it is a inventory management tool that allows merchants to update their inventories and form a collaborative database for local products.
  • In between the web browser extension and the inventory management tool is a website which serves as an online marketplace that connects online shoppers and local merchants.

Value Propositions

For Online Shoppers

  • Glocal web browser extension actively complements your online search queries on shoppable websites
  • Glocal provides improved shopping experience e.g. in-store experience & same-day delivery.

For Local Merchants

  • Categories of goods: Books | Records | Apparel | Shoes | Accessories | Sportswear - Based on interviews with shoppers and merchants, time-sensitive and experience are the primary reasons people purchase locally.
  • Glocal helps increase online visibility
  • Glocal helps enlarge customer base both online and in-store.
  • Glocal provides consolidated inventory management.
