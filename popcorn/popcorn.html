The Popcorn Factory

Decile Analysis, Fixed-Basis Segmentation, Clustering, Market Response Model

BACKGROUND

It was a class project during my graduate program. The database of the project was provided by The popcorn Factory. We were asked to conduct segmentation and customer profiling and come up with targeted tactics and messages to different segments for the purpose of optimal revenue.

Best Customer Segments

Customer Identification

To identify the most profitable customers, we ran a decile analysis based on the revenue. The result proved that not all customers are equal, which provided rationale for segmention.

Customer Valuation

We conducted a clustering segmentation using 3 variables: Monetary value(Revenue),Recency, and Frequency. Among the 6 segments we created, we decided that segment 5 and 6 would be our primary focus.

Segment Profile

We named segment 5 The Caterpillars and segment 6 The Butterflies. Because The Caterpillars are slightly lower in monetary value and in purchase frequency than our best customers, The Butterflies. We further profiled the two focus segments based on their media usage, product preference, etc.

Targeted Marketing

Response Model

Based on response media history data, we created a market response model to calibrate the effectiveness of different marketing tactics.

Objectives & Budget

We set up behavioral and communications objectives for different segments and allocated marketing budget towards each.

Tactics & Messages

We developed optimal marketing tactics and messages for each segments based on the market response model and marketing objectives.
